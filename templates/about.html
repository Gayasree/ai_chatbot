About the Project

The Mission

This project was built to tackle the challenges of passive learning in higher education. We aim to reduce cognitive load by providing interactive, real-time academic support.

The Team

Developed by a group of passionate Computer Science students dedicated to making education more accessible and engaging through AI.

Technology

Built using Flask, PyPDF2, and modern web technologies to ensure a scalable and efficient learning support system.
